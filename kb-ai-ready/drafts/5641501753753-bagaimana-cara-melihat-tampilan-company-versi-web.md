## Prerequisites  <!-- confidence:medium ~ -->

• Active Qontak CRM account
• Access to web version of Qontak CRM
• User permissions to view Companies menu
• Internet connection and supported web browser

## Steps  <!-- confidence:high ✓ -->

1. Log in to your Qontak CRM account
2. Click **Companies** menu from main navigation
3. View the Companies page with all available features displayed:
   - Filter: Filter companies by owner or team
   - Search: Find companies by name
   - Show: Set number of companies displayed per page
   - Add Company: Create new companies
   - Download: Export company data
   - Bulk Change: Transfer companies to another owner
   - Delete: Remove companies (bulk or individual)
4. Review columns: Company Name, Associated With, Type, Owner/Created, Updated dates, and action buttons (Edit/Delete)

## Expected Result  <!-- confidence:high ✓ -->

The Companies page displays with a table listing all your companies. You can see:
• Complete company database with names, types, owners, and dates
• All action buttons and filters available for company management
• Pagination controls showing total pages of companies
• Search and filter options ready to use

## Error States  <!-- confidence:high ✓ -->

No common errors documented.

## Escalation  <!-- confidence:medium ~ -->

If the Companies menu is not visible or the page fails to load:
• Verify your user account has Companies module permissions
• Clear browser cache and refresh the page
• Try accessing from a different browser
• Contact Qontak support with: account email, screenshot of the issue, and browser/device details
