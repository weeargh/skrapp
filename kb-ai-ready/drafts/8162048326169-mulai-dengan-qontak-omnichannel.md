## Definition  <!-- confidence:medium ~ -->

Qontak Omnichannel is a unified messaging platform that integrates multiple communication channels into a single inbox. It enables businesses to manage customer conversations across web, mobile, WhatsApp, and other channels from one centralized interface, streamlining communication workflows and improving customer service efficiency.

## Why It Matters  <!-- confidence:medium ~ -->

Omnichannel support helps businesses provide consistent, responsive customer service across all touchpoints. By consolidating messages into one inbox, teams reduce response times, prevent missed conversations, and improve customer satisfaction. Integration with Qontak Service Suite enables better ticket management and agent collaboration, ultimately boosting operational efficiency and customer loyalty.

## Key Attributes  <!-- confidence:medium ~ -->

• Multi-channel message consolidation (web, mobile, WhatsApp)
• Unified inbox with room history and message filtering
• Automated message routing to agents
• Chat templates and blast messaging capabilities
• Real-time online/offline status management
• AI-powered assistance (Mekari Airene)
• 24-hour open chat sessions
• Tag and note assignment for message organization
• Agent handover and collaboration tools
• Media sharing support
