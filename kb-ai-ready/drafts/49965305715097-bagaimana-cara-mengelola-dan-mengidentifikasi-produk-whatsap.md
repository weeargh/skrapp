## Prerequisites  <!-- confidence:high ✓ -->

1. Active Facebook account connected to Commerce Manager (business.facebook.com/commerce)
2. Access to Business Manager (business.facebook.com/settings)
3. WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) connected to Qontak Omnichannel
4. Qontak Chat or Qontak Omnichannel plan activated
5. Note: Catalog creation occurs in Commerce Manager, not in Qontak platform

## Steps  <!-- confidence:high ✓ -->

1. Log in to Commerce Manager with Facebook credentials
2. Click 'Add products' to start catalog creation
3. Set Catalogue type, Business ID, and catalog name
4. Create Business ID if needed (fill business name, your name, business email → Submit → verify email link)
5. Click 'Next'
6. Connect Meta pixel or app SDK by clicking 'Connect a new event source' and 'Save' (or skip if setting up later)
7. Connect catalog to WhatsApp Business Account in Business Manager
8. Customers can now view and click products in catalog from WhatsApp Business profile

## Expected Result  <!-- confidence:high ✓ -->

Catalog successfully created and connected to WhatsApp Business Account. Customers can:
- View products directly in WhatsApp Business profile
- Click and interact with catalog items
- Send messages referencing specific products

Business users can identify products referenced by customers from the catalog within Qontak Inbox.

## Error States  <!-- confidence:high ✓ -->

No common errors documented.

## Escalation  <!-- confidence:medium ~ -->

Contact Qontak support if:
- Catalog does not appear in WhatsApp Business profile after connection
- Customers cannot view or interact with catalog products
- Multiple WABAs not syncing to same catalog as expected

Provide: Screenshot of Commerce Manager setup, WABA account details, and Qontak account ID.
