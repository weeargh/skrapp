## Definition  <!-- confidence:medium ~ -->

Qontak CRM is a comprehensive customer relationship management system designed to help businesses manage customer interactions, sales pipelines, and customer data in one unified platform. It enables teams to organize contacts, track deals, manage tickets, and monitor performance through integrated dashboards and reporting tools.

## Why It Matters  <!-- confidence:medium ~ -->

Qontak CRM helps businesses streamline customer management by centralizing contact information, automating sales workflows, and enabling data-driven decision-making. It improves team collaboration, reduces response times through ticket management and SLA policies, and provides visibility into customer interactions across multiple channels.

## Key Attributes  <!-- confidence:medium ~ -->

• Centralized contact and company database
• Ticket management with SLA policies
• Customizable sales pipelines
• Dashboard reporting and analytics
• Multi-channel communication support
• Mobile and web access
• Bulk and single data entry options
• Configurable workflow automation
